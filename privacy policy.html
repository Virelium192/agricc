Privacy Policy

Your trust is our priority. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your agricultural data to help you farm smarter while keeping your information secure.

Last Updated: March 08, 2026 Version 2.4

Table of Contents

1. Introduction

AgriClimate Connect ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered agricultural platform, mobile application, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

We understand that agricultural data is sensitive and valuable. Whether you are a smallholder farmer in Kenya, a commercial operator in Brazil, or an agricultural researcher, your data privacy is paramount to us. This policy applies to all users worldwide who access our Services.

By accessing or using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services.

This policy complies with major data protection regulations including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for European users, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California residents, and other applicable local laws in the 120+ countries where we operate.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account (name, email, phone number)
  • Complete your farm profile (location, farm size, crop types)
  • Participate in community forums or expert consultations
  • Contact our support team or agricultural experts
  • Upload images for pest/disease diagnosis
  • Connect with market buyers or sellers

2.2 Agricultural & Technical Data

To provide our AI-powered recommendations, we collect:

Geolocation Data

GPS coordinates for hyperlocal weather forecasts and soil analysis.

Image Data

Photos of crops, pests, and soil for computer vision analysis.

Sensor Data

IoT device readings for soil moisture, temperature, and humidity.

2.3 Automatically Collected Data

When you access our Services, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage patterns, including IP address, browser type, operating system, app usage statistics, and cookies. This helps us improve our AI models and user experience.

3. How We Use Your Data

1

Service Provision

To provide personalized crop recommendations, weather alerts, and market intelligence tailored to your specific location and farming conditions.

2

AI Model Training

To train and improve our machine learning models for pest detection, yield prediction, and climate adaptation strategies. All training data is anonymized.

3

Community Features

To facilitate connections between farmers, experts, and buyers within our global agricultural network.

4

Research & Innovation

To conduct agricultural research that benefits the global farming community, particularly regarding climate change adaptation.

5

Safety & Security

To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or security breaches that could compromise your data.

6

Communication

To send you service updates, critical weather alerts, and marketing communications (which you can opt out of).

4. Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Our platform utilizes advanced Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) algorithms to analyze agricultural data and provide recommendations. This section explains how we handle data in our AI systems.

Computer Vision for Crop Analysis

When you upload images of your crops, pests, or diseases, our AI analyzes these images to provide diagnosis and treatment recommendations.

  • Images are processed in real-time using encrypted connections
  • Original images are stored securely for your records
  • Anonymized image features may be used to improve our disease detection models
  • You retain full ownership of your images

Predictive Analytics & Climate Models

We aggregate anonymized data from millions of farms to build predictive models for weather patterns, crop yields, and market trends.

Individual farm data is never sold to third parties
Aggregated insights help smallholder farmers compete
You can opt-out of model training contributions
All AI decisions can be reviewed by human experts

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers

We engage trusted third-party companies to perform functions on our behalf, such as cloud hosting (AWS, Google Cloud), payment processing, and SMS notification services. These providers have access only to the data necessary to perform their functions and are contractually obligated to protect your data.

Marketplace Connections

When you choose to connect with buyers or sellers through our Market Intelligence feature, we share your contact information and farm produce details with the specific party you choose to connect with. You control these connections.

Research Partners

We may share anonymized, aggregated data with universities and agricultural research institutions to advance climate-smart agriculture. No personally identifiable information is included in these datasets.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

6. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our security infrastructure includes:

Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Your agricultural secrets are as secure as banking data.

Authentication

Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for all accounts. We support biometric login on mobile devices.

Access Control

Strict role-based access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access specific data types.

Auditing

Continuous monitoring and regular security audits by third-party firms ensure compliance with ISO 27001 standards.

Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours as required by GDPR and other applicable regulations.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you have certain rights regarding your personal data. We are committed to facilitating these rights:

Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification

Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten").

Right to Restrict Processing

Limit how we use your data while retaining the information.

Right to Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Right to Object

Opt-out of data processing for direct marketing or AI training.

To exercise these rights: Log into your account settings or contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@agriclimate.com. We respond to all requests within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active. Deleted 2 years after account closure unless legally required to keep longer.
  • Agricultural Data: Historical crop and weather data retained for 7 years to provide long-term trend analysis and improve AI models.
  • Images: Stored for 1 year unless you delete them earlier. Anonymized versions may be kept for model training.
  • Communication Records: Support tickets and chat logs kept for 3 years for quality assurance.

You can request early deletion of specific data types at any time through your account dashboard.

9. International Data Transfers

AgriClimate Connect operates globally. Your information may be transferred to — and maintained on — computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where data protection laws may differ.

Specifically, our primary servers are located in Ireland (EU) and the United States. We ensure adequate protection for international transfers through:

Standard Contractual Clauses

EU-approved data transfer agreements

Data Privacy Framework

Certified under EU-US Data Privacy Framework

Regional Servers

Data residency options for enterprise clients

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors.

Notification Process: We will notify you of any material changes via email (sent to the address associated with your account) or through a prominent notice on our platform at least 30 days before the changes become effective.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page, with the "Last Updated" date at the top modified accordingly.

Continued use of our Services after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
