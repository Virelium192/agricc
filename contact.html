Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For technical support, our average response time is under 4 hours. Premium support subscribers receive responses within 1 hour. Live chat is available for immediate assistance during support hours.

Absolutely! For partnership discussions, enterprise sales, or media interviews, you can request a scheduled call through our contact form. Select the appropriate subject and mention your preferred time slots. Our team will send you a calendar invitation with a video meeting link.

We have regional hubs in Kenya (East Africa), Nigeria (West Africa), India (South Asia), and Brazil (Latin America). Additionally, we have field officers and partner organizations in over 40 countries. Contact us with your location, and we'll connect you with the nearest representative.

For technical issues, please email support@agriclimate.org with details about the problem, including screenshots if possible. You can also use the live chat for immediate assistance. For critical issues affecting your farming operations, mark your message as "Urgent" and we'll prioritize it.

We welcome partnerships with NGOs, government agencies, research institutions, and agribusinesses. Please email partners@agriclimate.org with a brief description of your organization and collaboration goals. Our partnerships team will schedule an introductory call to explore synergies and next steps.
