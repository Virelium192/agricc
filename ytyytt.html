Creating an account is simple and takes less than 2 minutes:

  1. Click the "Get Started" button in the top right corner
  2. Enter your email address and create a password
  3. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox
  4. Complete your profile with basic farm information
  5. Start exploring the platform features

Tip: You can also sign up using your Google account for faster registration.
