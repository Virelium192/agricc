Official Community Standards

Community Guidelines

Creating a safe, respectful, inclusive, and knowledge-sharing space for farmers, agronomists, researchers, and agri-entrepreneurs worldwide.

Our Five Core Principles

These foundational values shape every interaction, decision, and policy in the AgriClimate Connect community.

Respect & Dignity

Every member deserves to be treated with courtesy, regardless of nationality, farm size, experience level, gender, religion, or farming philosophy.

Use inclusive, non-derogatory language at all times

Honor cultural differences in agricultural practices

Never mock, shame, or belittle questions or opinions

No personal attacks, slurs, or targeted harassment

Open Knowledge Sharing

Freely share experience and expertise while remaining humble and open to correction and new learning.

Provide practical, evidence-based advice whenever possible

Cite sources or personal experience when sharing technical info

Share both successes and honest failures — real learning comes from both

Do not gatekeep knowledge or promote only proprietary solutions

Safety & Responsibility

Prioritize human health, animal welfare, environmental protection, and economic viability in every recommendation.

Clearly warn about risks of chemicals, machinery, or practices

Promote regenerative, climate-resilient, and biodiversity-friendly methods

Never give dangerous, illegal, or banned practice advice

No promotion of pesticides/inputs that are banned in the EU, WHO Class Ia/Ib, etc.

Integrity & Transparency

Be honest about your expertise, affiliations, limitations, and any commercial interests.

Clearly disclose if you work for, sell, or receive benefits from any recommended product/company

Say "I don't know" or "consult a local expert" when unsure

Correct your own mistakes promptly when pointed out

No undisclosed affiliate links, fake reviews, or astroturfing

Collaboration & Support

Help each other succeed — celebrate wins, offer help without judgment, and build bridges across borders and scales.

Offer constructive feedback and solutions, not just criticism

Celebrate fellow farmers' successes and milestones

Connect people who can help each other (buyers, suppliers, mentors)

No gatekeeping, elitism, or exclusionary behavior

Our Community Pledge

By participating in AgriClimate Connect, I commit to upholding these five principles, treating others with respect, sharing knowledge responsibly, prioritizing safety, acting with integrity, and fostering collaboration for the benefit of farmers everywhere.

Detailed Code of Conduct

Specific behavioral expectations across different parts of the platform.

Forum & Discussion Standards

Stay Relevant & Use Tags

Post in the correct category and use appropriate tags. Avoid thread hijacking or off-topic replies.

Constructive Criticism Only

Critique ideas and methods — never attack people. Offer solutions together with problems.

Search First – Reduce Duplicates

Use search before posting. Add to existing threads when appropriate instead of starting identical ones.

Protect Privacy

Never share screenshots of private messages, personal details, or farm locations without explicit consent.

Strictly Prohibited

  • Spam, self-promotion, affiliate link flooding, repetitive posting
  • Harassment, doxxing, threats, bullying, targeted insults
  • Hate speech, slurs, discrimination based on protected characteristics
  • Deliberate misinformation presented as fact (especially health/safety claims)
  • Sock-puppeting, vote manipulation, brigading

Encouraged Behaviors

  • Use descriptive thread titles and include photos/videos when asking for help
  • Return to threads to post updates and final outcomes
  • Welcome and patiently onboard new members
  • Use the "Thank", "Helpful", and "Solution" reactions generously

Expert Consultation Standards

Farmers Seeking Advice

  • Provide full context: location, soil type, climate, current practices, photos
  • Be respectful of experts' time — ask focused, specific questions
  • Implement advice safely and report back results (good or bad)
  • Understand remote advice cannot replace local professional consultation

Certified Experts & Contributors

  • Clearly state your qualifications, experience, and any limitations
  • Disclose commercial relationships or conflicts of interest
  • Recommend local lab testing / vet / agronomist when needed
  • Stay current with research and update advice accordingly

Quality & Ethics Standards

Response Time

Experts should aim to respond within 48 hours (business days)

Record Keeping

Experts encouraged to keep notes for continuity and liability protection

Liability Disclaimer

All remote advice is general — farmers remain responsible for final decisions

Transparent Listings

Accurate photos, honest descriptions, realistic pricing, clear condition & location

Fair & Respectful Negotiation

No pressure tactics, fake offers, lowballing with intent to insult, or ghosting after agreement

Secure & Traceable Transactions

Use platform escrow when available, verified payment methods, keep communication on-platform

Strictly Prohibited in Marketplace

Counterfeit / Fake Inputs

Fake seeds, expired chemicals, cloned equipment, forged certificates

CITES / Endangered Species

Protected plants, animals, or derivatives without proper permits

Price Fixing / Collusion

Group agreements to artificially inflate/deflate prices or restrict supply

Banned / Restricted Chemicals

WHO Class Ia/Ib, Rotterdam Convention, Stockholm POPs, EU-banned substances

Scams & Fraud

Advance-fee fraud, fake escrow, phishing links, impersonation

Off-Platform Pressure

Forcing buyers/sellers to move to WhatsApp/Telegram before transaction completion

Intellectual Property & Attribution Rules

Original Content

You retain ownership of photos, videos, text, and data you post. By uploading, you grant AgriClimate Connect a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free license to display, distribute, and promote your content within the platform and related marketing (with attribution where practical).

Third-Party Content

Only share content you own, have written permission to share, or that clearly falls under fair use / fair dealing for educational/scientific discussion. Always credit photographers, researchers, publications, or platforms.

Prohibited Content Types

Graphic / Disturbing Media

Excessive gore, animal cruelty, graphic pesticide poisoning, or violent content (even if educational)

Misleading / Manipulated Media

Deepfakes, heavily edited images presented as real, out-of-context screenshots designed to mislead

Copyright Infringement

Reposting entire research papers, books, paywalled articles, commercial videos without permission

NSFW / Inappropriate Content

Sexually explicit material, nudity, extreme political/religious content unrelated to agriculture

Enforcement & Appeals Process

How we maintain community standards while ensuring fairness and due process.

Enforcement Approach

Tiered Response System

Most first offenses receive warnings or temporary mutes. Serious or repeated violations result in temporary or permanent bans.

Review Timeline

Moderation team aims to review reports within 72 hours. Critical safety violations are addressed within 24 hours.

Appeals Process

Users may appeal moderation actions within 30 days via the in-app appeal form or email appeals@agriclimateconnect.com
