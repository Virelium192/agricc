Our Story

AgriClimate Connect was born from a simple yet powerful observation: while climate change was increasingly threatening global food security, smallholder farmers—who produce 70% of the world's food—lacked access to the information and tools needed to adapt.

Founded in 2018 by a team of agricultural scientists and technology entrepreneurs in Nairobi, Kenya, we set out to bridge this gap. What started as a small pilot project serving 500 farmers in East Africa has grown into a global movement reaching 50,000 farmers across 120 countries.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation. From early SMS-based weather alerts to today's AI-powered comprehensive platform, we've remained committed to our core belief: that knowledge, when made accessible, becomes the most powerful tool for transformation.

We currently serve farmers in 120 countries, with strongest presence in East Africa, South Asia, and West Africa. Our platform supports 15 languages and we're constantly expanding. If your country isn't fully supported yet, you can still access many of our features and join our global community.

We welcome agricultural experts, researchers, and experienced farmers to join our expert network. Apply through our website with your credentials and areas of expertise. Once approved, you can host webinars, create courses, answer farmer questions, and earn income while helping others.

Data privacy is paramount. We use enterprise-grade encryption, never sell personal data, and comply with GDPR and local data protection laws. Farmers control what information they share, and we use aggregated, anonymized data only for improving our AI models and generating regional insights.
